// About BelFed Analytics

A disciplined framework for finding edge — and capturing it.

BelFed Analytics is a research-driven trading intelligence service for those seeking consistent edge and balanced risk-reward opportunities across global financial markets.

We are a new service built around one core purpose: delivering in-house market analysis and research — alongside curated insights from top-tier industry firms — for those who seek clarity on where global financial markets currently stand and where the best opportunities lie.

Our process combines macro research with technical analysis to identify high-probability setups across global markets. We study the broader economic backdrop, monitor cross-asset signals, and use price action to refine timing and execution.

We also believe successful trading requires more than strong research. Discipline, psychology, and risk management are essential to long-term performance, which is why our work is grounded in both deep analytical thinking and practical decision-making.

Our mission is simple: to deliver clear research, actionable trade ideas, and a disciplined framework for finding edge — and capturing it.

Artem Fedorov
Managing Partner & Chief Investment Officer
